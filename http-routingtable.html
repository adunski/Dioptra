HTTP Routing Table

/api
 
/api
GET /api/experiment/
GET /api/experiment/name/{experimentName} Gets an experiment by its unique name
GET /api/experiment/{experimentId} Gets an experiment by its unique identifier
GET /api/job/
GET /api/job/{jobId} Gets a job by its unique identifier
GET /api/queue/
GET /api/queue/name/{queueName} Gets a queue by its unique name
GET /api/queue/{queueId} Gets a queue by its unique identifier
GET /api/taskPlugin/
GET /api/taskPlugin/dioptra_builtins/{taskPluginName} Gets a builtin task plugin by its unique name
GET /api/taskPlugin/dioptra_custom/{taskPluginName} Gets a custom task plugin by its unique name
POST /api/experiment/
POST /api/job/
POST /api/queue/
POST /api/taskPlugin/
PUT /api/experiment/name/{experimentName} Modifies an experiment by its unique name
PUT /api/experiment/{experimentId} Modifies an experiment by its unique identifier
PUT /api/queue/name/{queueName} Modifies a queue by its unique name
PUT /api/queue/name/{queueName}/lock Locks the queue (name reference) if it is unlocked
PUT /api/queue/{queueId} Modifies a queue by its unique identifier
PUT /api/queue/{queueId}/lock Locks the queue (id reference) if it is unlocked
DELETE /api/experiment/name/{experimentName} Deletes an experiment by its unique name
DELETE /api/experiment/{experimentId} Deletes an experiment by its unique identifier
DELETE /api/queue/name/{queueName} Deletes a queue by its unique name
DELETE /api/queue/name/{queueName}/lock Removes the lock from the queue (name reference) if it exists
DELETE /api/queue/{queueId} Deletes a queue by its unique identifier
DELETE /api/queue/{queueId}/lock Removes the lock from the queue (id reference) if it exists
DELETE /api/taskPlugin/dioptra_custom/{taskPluginName} Deletes a custom task plugin by its unique name
